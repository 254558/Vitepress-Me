# 为什么大家都说人生的容错率大到无法想象？
为什么大家都说人生的容错率大到无法想象？

Why does everyone say that the margin for error in life is unimaginably high?

---
因为这是假的，老弟。

Because this is not true, buddy.

---
人生根本没什么容错率

There is basically no margin for error in life at all.

---
我国有极其严重的年龄歧视，学历歧视，阶级歧视

Our country has extremely severe age discrimination, academic discrimination and class discrimination.

---
且不说前22年的学业生涯有任何差池，自动被身边人视为万劫不复

Not to mention that any misstep in the first 22 years of academic life will automatically be regarded by people around you as a point of no return.

---
结错婚，意外怀孕，收到重大伤害（残疾），生病，投资失败，负债，选错专业，甚至在无良企业浪费了几年都对人生产生了不可磨灭的影响

Marrying the wrong person, unplanned pregnancy, suffering a major injury (resulting in disability), falling ill, failing in investments, getting into debt, choosing the wrong major, or even wasting a few years in an unscrupulous company—all these will exert an indelible impact on one’s life.

---
35岁以后只有求稳这一条路可走

After the age of 35, playing it safe is the only way to go.

---
然而22岁到35之间，能做的选择少之又少，要做的事又太多太多

Yet between the ages of 22 and 35, the number of choices one can make is extremely limited, while there are far too many things to do.

---
年轻人需要积攒人脉，资历，和能力

Young people need to accumulate connections, qualifications and capabilities.

---
那么就需要你毕业几年内决定自己这辈子深耕的领域

This means you have to decide the field you will dedicate yourself to for life within a few years after graduation.

---
一旦换行全部从零开始

Once you switch careers, you have to start all over from scratch.

---
硕士的人生从25岁开始，博士的人生从30岁开始

A master’s degree holder’s real life starts at 25, and a doctoral degree holder’s at 30.

---
我还没谈你能不能一次考上，也没谈你能不能毕业

I haven’t even touched on whether you can get admitted on the first try, or whether you can graduate at all.

---
在这期间没有任何收入，全部是家里承担

During this period, you have no income whatsoever, and all expenses are borne by your family.

---
并且博士硕士基本上没有改行换业的可能性了

Moreover, master’s and doctoral degree holders basically have no possibility of changing their careers.

---
任何选择都是赌博

Every choice is a gamble.

---
任何选择的收益不一定是你自己独享

The gains from any choice may not be enjoyed exclusively by yourself.

---
但是任何选择的失败后果全部由自己承担

But the consequences of failure from any choice are all borne by you alone.

---
连用个花呗都要三思而后行

You even have to think thrice before using Huabei.

---
你跟我说人生的容错率很高，思之令人发笑

Yet you tell me that the margin for error in life is high—it’s laughable to think about.

---
以下内容为增添

Addendum

---
我讲这话的意思不是说什么想着当人上人才容错率低

What I mean by saying this is not that the margin for error is low only if you want to be a cut above the rest.

---
你们对当前的社会现状是多么睁眼瞎

How blind are you to the current social reality?

---
难道觉得有一份朝九晚五双休的工作，月收入在柴米油盐，房租水电后还有点盈余，有一个对象，然后精神状态健康正常的生活，很容易得到的

Do you really think that it’s easy to get a 9-to-5 job with weekends off, have some surplus income after covering daily necessities, rent and utility bills, have a partner, and live a life with sound mental health?

---
而且你把我说的坑全避了，也成为不了人上人的

Moreover, even if you avoid all the pitfalls I mentioned, you still can’t be a cut above the rest.

---
你几辈子买得起230万的耳环

How many lifetimes will it take you to afford a pair of earrings worth 2.3 million yuan?
