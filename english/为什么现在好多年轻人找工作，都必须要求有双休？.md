# 为什么现在好多年轻人找工作，都必须要求有双休？
为什么现在好多年轻人找工作，都必须要求有双休？

Why do so many young people nowadays insist on having weekends off when looking for a job?

---
很多人都没有意识到，或者不愿意承认一个问题：

Many people fail to realize, or are unwilling to acknowledge, one thing:

---
双休不是目的，是底线

Weekends off are not a goal—they are the bottom line.

---
你是一个活生生的人

You are a living, breathing human being.

---
作为一个人，是需要有时间休息的

As a human being, you need time to rest.

---
作为一个人，是需要时间和精力去学会娱乐并且参与进去的

As a human being, you need the time and energy to learn how to have fun and actually engage in it.

---
作为一个人，是需要时间和精力陪伴家人的

As a human being, you need the time and energy to accompany your family.

---
作为一个人，是需要时间和精力学会并且参与和其他的人建立信任，成为朋友的

As a human being, you need the time and energy to learn how to build trust with others and become friends with them.

---
作为一个人，是需要时间和精力学会并且找到一个异性/同性/沃尔玛购物袋/武装直升机……总之就是另一个个体建立亲密关系，并培养感情的

As a human being, you need the time and energy to learn how to find another individual—be it someone of the opposite sex, the same sex, a Walmart shopping bag, an attack helicopter... whatever—and build an intimate, emotional bond with them.

---
作为一个人，是需要时间和精力去学习劳动技能的

As a human being, you need the time and energy to learn work skills.

---
作为一个人，是需要时间和精力去了解并且思考社会的

As a human being, you need the time and energy to understand and reflect on society.

---
作为一个人，是需要尊重和被尊重的

As a human being, you need to respect others and be respected in return.

---
但同时作为一个人

But at the same time, as a human being,

---
你的身体很脆弱，青春燃烧不了几年，伤病会伴随一生

your body is fragile. Your youth fades in just a few years, and injuries and illnesses will haunt you for life.

---
你的精神很脆弱，年少时受到的创伤，人到中年还能从梦里惊醒

your mind is fragile. Trauma suffered in your youth can still jolt you awake from dreams when you reach middle age.

---
你的精力更脆弱，全世界都会有意无意地消耗你的注意力，让你安心当一个只会简单劳动的人工智能

your energy is even more fragile. The whole world, consciously or unconsciously, drains your attention, lulling you into being nothing more than an AI that can only perform simple labor.

---
牛马扛不住日常化地十几个小时工作，但人可以

Draft animals can’t endure working over ten hours a day as a routine, but humans can.

---
机器大多扛不住全年无休，但人可以，而且会自己筹钱修自己

Most machines can’t run non-stop all year round, but humans can—and they’ll even scrape together money to "repair" themselves.

---
你有无数种方法，可以论证出一个结论：你不干有的是人干，996是真的福报

You can come up with countless arguments to reach the conclusion: If you don’t take the job, there are plenty of others who will; working 996 is truly a blessing.

---
但你不得不承认：但凡那个大型哺乳动物还想把自己当成人，最后都会发现学会当人是需要时间的，很多很多时间，所谓的双休只是最低标准罢了

But you have to admit this: Any of us large mammals who still want to be treated as humans will eventually realize that learning how to be a human takes time—a lot of time. The so-called weekends off are just the bare minimum.

---
大家只是穷，但大家不傻

People are just poor, not stupid.

---
大家只是没得选，不代表大家不想选

People just have no other options—it doesn’t mean they don’t want to choose.
