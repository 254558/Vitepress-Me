# 为什么有人会以为自杀是一种解脱？
为什么有人会以为自杀是一种解脱？

Why do some people think that suicide is a form of relief?

---
因为自杀确实是对所有问题的最高效解决方式。

Because suicide is indeed the most efficient way to resolve all problems.

---
它回避了一切问题，让一切责任都无法选中，让一切痛苦和攻击都无效化，也符合及时止损的原则。

It evades all problems, renders all responsibilities unenforceable, invalidates all pain and attacks, and also conforms to the principle of cutting losses in a timely manner.

---
此外，这也是最后的反抗策略，以生命为代价表达自己对世界的蔑视。

Furthermore, it is also a final act of resistance—a way to express one’s contempt for the world at the cost of one’s own life.

---
面对股市，上策是尽早离去；

When it comes to the stock market, the best strategy is to exit as early as possible;

---
面对人生，上策也是尽早离去。

When it comes to life, the best strategy is also to exit as early as possible.

---
只要活着，就难免会遇到问题，就难免会痛苦，就会有各种纠缠不清，只有死亡，才能剪断所有羁绊，得到真正的自由。

As long as you are alive, you will inevitably encounter problems, suffer pain, and get tangled in all kinds of messes. Only death can sever all fetters and grant you true freedom.

---
活着并不意味比死亡高贵，只是赌瘾太深，不舍得离去。

Being alive does not mean being more noble than death; it is merely that the addiction to gambling is too strong to bear leaving.

---
活着并不需要什么意义，上瘾后自然而然就活下去了。

Living does not require any meaning—once you are addicted, you will naturally keep going.

---
自杀者也不过是没了赌瘾，或者筹码输尽。

Those who commit suicide are simply those who have lost their gambling addiction, or who have lost all their chips.
