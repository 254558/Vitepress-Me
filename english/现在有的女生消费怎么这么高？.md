现在有的女生消费怎么这么高？

Why do some girls nowadays have such high consumption levels?

---
胡乱消费是智力问题，转移消费是道德问题，中产男唯一要注意的是，不要让这些变成自己的问题。

Reckless spending is a matter of poor judgment; shifting one’s financial burdens onto others is a matter of morality. The only thing middle-class men need to watch out for is not letting these issues become their own.
