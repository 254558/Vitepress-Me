# 你对不生孩子晚年会很凄惨的观点的看法？
你对不生孩子晚年会很凄惨的观点的看法？

What’s your take on the notion that people who don’t have kids will end up miserable in their old age?

---
我现在已经很凄惨了，没必要拿这个来吓我

I’m already having a miserable time right now—there’s no need to scare me with this.

---
对一个已经在地狱的人来说，你用地狱的可怕来吓唬他，这不是搞笑么？

To someone who’s already in hell, trying to frighten them with the horrors of hell—isn’t that just ridiculous?

---
第二点，我没那么乐观，一定认为自己会活到晚年生活不能自理的时候

Second, I’m not optimistic enough to assume I’ll definitely live to an old age where I’m unable to take care of myself.

---
第三点，我不生孩子，只有我一个人惨，生了孩子，全家都跟着惨。

Third, if I don’t have kids, only I’ll suffer. But if I do have kids, my whole family will suffer along with me.
