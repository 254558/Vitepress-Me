# 普通中产辛辛苦苦一辈子，然后房价暴跌，那么中产努力的意义在哪里？
普通中产辛辛苦苦一辈子，然后房价暴跌，那么中产努力的意义在哪里？

Ordinary middle-class people work their fingers to the bone their whole lives, only to see housing prices plummet—so what’s the point of all their hard work?

---
《我是刚需，不买房住哪里》

*I’m a genuine buyer—where else would I live if I don’t buy a house?*

---
《结婚不买房，安全感从哪里来》

*No house when getting married—where would a sense of security come from?*

---
《孩子要读书，不买房如何上学》

*Kids need to go to school—how can they enroll without owning a house?*

---
《租房不能随意添置家具，不买房哪来的生活品质》

*You can’t decorate a rental with furniture freely—where’s the quality of life without a house?*

---
《现金留在手上只会贬值，唯有买房才能保值升值》

*Cash in hand will only depreciate—only buying a house can preserve and increase its value.*

---
《政府不会允许房价下跌的，不然社会就乱了》

*The government will never let housing prices drop—otherwise, society will be thrown into chaos.*

---
《年轻的时候不买房，老了都没人把房子租给你》

*If you don’t buy a house when you’re young, no one will rent to you when you’re old.*

---
《说房价会下跌的都是买不起房子的loser》

*Anyone who says housing prices will fall is just a loser who can’t afford a house.*

---
《买房是普通人跨越阶层的唯一机会》

*Buying a house is the only chance for ordinary people to climb the social ladder.*

---
《地价越拍越高，面粉贵过面包，房价不可能跌》

*Land prices keep soaring at auctions—when flour is pricier than bread, housing prices can never drop.*

---
《一二线城市的房价不可能跌》

*Housing prices in first and second-tier cities will never fall.*

---
《一线城市的房价不可能跌》

*Housing prices in first-tier cities will never fall.*

---
《一线城市核心区的房价不可能跌》

*Housing prices in the core areas of first-tier cities will never fall.*

---
《一线城市核心区的豪宅不可能跌》

*Luxury home prices in the core areas of first-tier cities will never fall.*

---
《一线城市的房价不可能一直跌》

*Housing prices in first-tier cities can’t keep falling forever.*

---
《掏空所有买房，两年不到赔光首付，与自己和解》

*Empty every penny to buy a house, only to lose the entire down payment in less than two years—finally make peace with oneself.*
