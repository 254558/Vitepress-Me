# 上面本意是好的，只是下面人执行出错了。这句话有道理吗？
上面本意是好的，只是下面人执行出错了。这句话有道理吗？

Is there any truth to the saying “The higher-ups meant well, but those at the grassroots level messed up the execution”?

---
这句话还有另外配套的一半：我也没有办法，这是领导的要求。

This saying has a matching counterpart: “There’s nothing I can do about it—it’s the leader’s order.”

---
“上面的本意是好的，下面的人执行错了”+“我也没有办法，这是领导的要求”；这样下面的人和上面的人就都没有责任了。

Combine “The higher-ups meant well, but those at the grassroots level messed up the execution” with “There’s nothing I can do about it—it’s the leader’s order”—and just like that, neither those below nor those above bear any responsibility.
