# 大家都在争论彩礼，难道忘了还有嫁妆这回事吗？
大家都在争论彩礼，难道忘了还有嫁妆这回事吗？

Everyone is arguing about betrothal gifts—have we all forgotten about dowries?

---
因为嫁妆就是纯骗局

Because dowries are nothing but a complete scam.

---
嫁妆只有一个作用“抬高彩礼”，剩下的没了

A dowry serves only one purpose: to drive up the amount of the betrothal gift. Nothing more, nothing less.

---
嫁妆是结婚登记前女方父母赠予女方的婚前财产，说白了这钱哪怕给一个亿，那也就是女方家庭内部左手倒右手，跟男方毫无关系

A dowry is the premarital property gifted to the bride by her parents before marriage registration. To put it bluntly, even if this sum were 100 million yuan, it would just be moving money from one hand to the other within the bride’s family, having absolutely nothing to do with the groom.

---
唯一的作用是“我家都出了这么多嫁妆了，你家好意思就给这么点儿彩礼？”

Its sole function is to say: “We’ve given such a generous dowry—how can you be so stingy with the betrothal gift?”

---
然后多趁点儿彩礼钱

And then to squeeze more money out of the betrothal gift.

---
我家给了20万彩礼，你家出了20万嫁妆
My family gave a 200,000-yuan betrothal gift, and your family gave a 200,000-yuan dowry.

---
翻译过来的意思是
What this actually means is:

---
女方婚前得到了父母的20万赠予，同时又得到了男方20万的赠予
The bride receives a 200,000-yuan gift from her parents before marriage, and at the same time, another 200,000-yuan gift from the groom’s side.
