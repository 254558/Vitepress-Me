# 问所有男生一个问题，你们觉得28万的彩礼多吗？
问所有男生一个问题，你们觉得28万的彩礼多吗？
Let me ask all the guys a question: Do you think 280,000 yuan in betrothal gifts is a lot?

---
我至今不懂给彩礼的底层逻辑。
To this day, I still don’t understand the underlying logic behind giving betrothal gifts.

---
如果是讨个彩头，那我还能理解，大家给个吉利数字，万里挑一，6666，8888，这我能理解。
If it’s just for a lucky omen, I can get it—everyone chips in an auspicious amount, like “a needle in a haystack” (10,001 yuan), 6,666 yuan, or 8,888 yuan. That makes sense to me.

---
但是你要28万，请问这笔钱是干什么？
But if you ask for 280,000 yuan, may I ask what this sum is actually for?

---
如果是给亲朋好友看，那电商有那种伪装成一摞钱的玩具，你买几个放着，多有面子？但是你非要28万的钱，现金转账都可以，这又是为什么？
If it’s just to show off to relatives and friends, online stores sell fake stacks of money as toys—buy a few and display them, won’t that be just as face-saving? But you insist on 280,000 yuan in real money, either cash or transfer. Why is that?

---
如果说你父母养你那么大不容易，但是我父母养我那么大也不容易啊？你怎么不补偿我父母呢？我父母养我是应该的，你父母养你就要补偿？那我是赔钱货？
If you say your parents raised you with great difficulty, well, my parents raised me just as hard! Why don’t you compensate my parents then? Am I supposed to believe that my parents’ effort was “duty-bound”, but yours deserve a payout? Does that make me a “money-losing burden”?

---
如果说是为了给小家投资，那直接给儿子行不行？既然都是投资，为什么要过你们家那一手？如果是为了表达信任，那你们家的信任又在哪儿？
If it’s for investing in the new family, why not give the money directly to the couple? Since it’s an investment either way, why does it have to pass through your family’s hands first? If this is supposed to show trust, then where’s your family’s trust in us?
