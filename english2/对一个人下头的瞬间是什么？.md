# 对一个人下头的瞬间是什么？
对一个人下头的瞬间是什么？

What’s the exact moment you lose interest in someone?

---
7月5日相亲认识了一个女人，她和我聊女权主义、女性觉醒、社会对女性的不公平、女性的社会困境、女性运动的发展以及男女平等，女人要觉醒，要独立。

I met a woman on a blind date on July 5th. She talked to me about feminism, women’s awakening, societal unfairness toward women, the social predicaments women face, the development of the women’s movement, and gender equality—saying women need to wake up and be independent.

---
7月6日她问我能出多少彩礼三金，房子能不能加名。

On July 6th, she asked me how much I could afford for betrothal gifts and the three types of gold jewelry, and whether she could be added to the house title.
