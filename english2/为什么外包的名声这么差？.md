# 为什么外包的名声这么差？
为什么外包的名声这么差？

Why does outsourcing have such a bad reputation?

---
果然人不能吃太饱，吃太饱了，干啥事儿都有人指指点点

People shouldn’t have too much leisure time; when they do, they’ll nitpick whatever others do.

---
你以为去外包的那些人不想去正式公司上班？不想着全额的五险一金？不想着一年13薪14薪？不想有各种补贴？

Do you think those who work at outsourcing firms don’t want to get jobs at regular companies? Don’t they want full social insurance and housing fund benefits? Don’t they want 13th or 14th month salaries? Don’t they want various subsidies?

---
他们想被剥削一层？

Do they actually want to be exploited an extra layer?

---
有些人能力or学历达不到，总不能让这些达不到能力的人就不活了吧？

Some people just don’t have the required skills or educational qualifications—but we can’t just let them starve, can we?

---
有的去了外包连自己的门禁卡都没有，还得蹭人家的，有的甚至连工位都没有，哪是空的去哪里坐着

Some don’t even get their own access cards at outsourcing companies and have to piggyback on others’; some don’t even have a fixed workstation and just sit wherever there’s an empty spot.

---
还得被你们这帮SB指指点点

And they still have to put up with the judgmental crap from idiots like you.

---
这个时代能活下去就很艰难了，你要是闲的没事儿，自己去咬打火机去好吗？

It’s already hard enough just to survive in this day and age. If you’ve got nothing better to do, why don’t you go find something pointless to occupy yourself with?

---
我在跟你讲活着很难，你跟我讲外包对人的剥削，都要活不下去了，我在乎这个？

I’m telling you how tough it is just to get by, and you’re lecturing me about exploitation in outsourcing. When someone’s barely clinging to survival, do you think they care about that?

---
再说了，我在乎能有用吗？我难道不知道被剥削？

Besides, what good would it do even if I did care? Do you think I don’t know I’m being exploited?

---
不在一个频道上就别来留言了，我说话比较脏，回复不了。

If we’re not on the same page, don’t bother commenting. I’m not holding back with my language, and I won’t bother replying.
