# 我不理解为啥有的男生只要被拒绝就一下冷淡了？
我不理解为啥有的男生只要被拒绝就一下冷淡了？

I don’t get why some guys turn cold the second they get rejected?

---
很多女生无法理解一个东西。

There’s one thing a lot of girls just can’t wrap their heads around.

---
那就是在男生眼里，你就是一个普普通通的人。灵魂既不有趣，也没啥亮点和魅力。

And that is: in a guy’s eyes, you’re just an ordinary person—nothing interesting about your soul, no sparkle or charm to speak of.

---
除非他对你有意思，说难听点就是发情了，那此时你就才特殊起来。

Unless he’s into you—to put it bluntly, unless he’s attracted to you—only then do you become special to him.

---
然后你拒绝了他，在他了解你们不可能后，你又变回了满大街遍地走的普通人。

Then you reject him. Once he realizes there’s no chance between you two, you go right back to being just another face in the crowd.

---
换位思考，他为什么要对一个普通人继续热情，是精力使不完吗？

Put yourself in his shoes. Why would he keep being enthusiastic about an ordinary person? Does he have way too much energy to burn?

---
你也说他热情幽默开朗会说话，那他会缺你这个聊天搭子吗？

You yourself said he’s outgoing, funny, cheerful and a good talker—so do you really think he’s short of someone to chat with?
