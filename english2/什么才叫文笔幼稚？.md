# 什么才叫文笔幼稚？
What does "childish writing style" actually mean?

---
幼稚文笔：
Childish writing:

---
商店的霓虹灯光洒在地上，不停变换，仿佛一颗颗鲜艳诱人的糖果。
The store’s neon lights spilled onto the ground, shifting constantly—like bright, tempting candies.

---
不幼稚文笔：
Non-childish writing:

---
没有怎么刷完这位up的视频，
I didn’t finish watching this creator’s video,

---
但是我也曾经在西雅图工作过几年，
but I used to work in Seattle for a few years.

---
租住的地方附近的街道流浪汉也很多，
The streets near where I rented had a lot of homeless people,

---
由于大部分人精神都不太正常，我也不太敢接近，
most seemed mentally unstable, so I didn’t dare get close.

---
但是至今我也记得一个场景，
But I still remember a scene,

---
大概是21年的圣诞节晚上，我出去买东西，
probably from Christmas Eve 2021: I was out shopping,

---
路过一名流浪汉，不停地捡地上的东西往嘴里塞，
and I passed a homeless person who kept picking up "things" from the ground to stuff into their mouth.

---
但是地上其实什么也没有，
But there was nothing there—

---
只有商店霓虹灯撒下来不停变换的光斑，
only the shifting spots of light from the store’s neon signs,

---
仿佛是一颗颗鲜艳的糖果
like bright candies.
