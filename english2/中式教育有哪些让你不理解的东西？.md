# 中式教育有哪些让你不理解的东西？
中式教育有哪些让你不理解的东西？

What aspects of Chinese-style education do you find incomprehensible?

---
不承认：时代、出生地、家境、智商、运气都比孩子个人的勤奋努力重要太多。

It refuses to acknowledge this truth: era, place of birth, family background, intelligence, and luck are far more important than a child’s personal diligence and efforts.

---
对人性和个人主观能动性抱有不切实际的期待，妄想让孩子在有限的时间里靠个人努力学习去弥补客观条件导致的差距。

It holds unrealistic expectations for human nature and individual subjective initiative, deluding itself that children can make up for the gaps caused by objective conditions through personal academic efforts within a limited time frame.
