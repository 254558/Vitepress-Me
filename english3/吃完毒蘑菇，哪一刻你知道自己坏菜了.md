吃完毒蘑菇，哪一刻你知道自己坏菜了？

After eating poisonous mushrooms, at what moment do you realize you’re doomed?

---

你以为想要得到的答案：

The answers you expect to get:

---

家里狗说话了；

Your family dog starts talking;

---

家里猫做饭了；

Your pet cat cooks meals;

---

路边树跳舞了；

Trees by the road start dancing;

---

街上车长腿了。

Cars on the street grow legs and walk.

---

实际更多的情况是：

The far more realistic situations are:

---

出院结账几万块没了；

You lose tens of thousands of yuan on medical bills after being discharged;

---

通知肾损需要定期透析；

You are told you have kidney damage and need regular dialysis;

---

神经彻底损伤终生嘴眼歪斜；

Permanent nerve damage leaves your mouth and eyes crooked for life;

---

亲人接到电话咋吃顿饭人就没了。

Your relatives get a phone call—someone who just had a meal suddenly passes away.

---

你以为去医院是漂亮护士姐姐掏出一瓶子解药，手上一扎，几个小时后你没事了。

You think going to the hospital means a pretty nurse takes out an antidote, gives you an injection, and you’ll be fine in a few hours.

---

实际很多毒蘑菇包括见手青，根本就没解毒剂，治疗方案多数只能血液净化加胆囊穿刺引流。

In fact, many poisonous mushrooms, including Jianshouqing, have no specific antidote at all. Most treatments only rely on blood purification plus gallbladder puncture and drainage.

---

没解毒剂啥概念？你蘑菇中毒，旁边一人被眼镜王蛇咬了，你比他难救。

What does having no antidote mean? If you get mushroom poisoning while someone beside you is bitten by a king cobra, you are harder to save than him.

---

你以为你住院是把你救回来就拍拍屁股回家吹牛逼去了，然后医保给你结账自己自费个千八百就行了？

Do you think staying in hospital means you get cured, go home casually to show off, and only pay a thousand or so yuan out of pocket with medical insurance covering the rest?

---

实际住院那钱只为了尽量清除你消化系统残存的毒素和让你已经坏了的零件别再扩大影响了。

In reality, all that hospitalization cost only serves to clear residual toxins in your digestive system and prevent damaged organs from getting worse.

---

肾损不可逆，肾小球肾小管这东西不会再生，你在你妈肚子里长的那些要用一辈子，坏一个少一个。

Kidney damage is irreversible. Glomeruli and renal tubules can never regenerate. The ones you develop before birth have to last a lifetime—once damaged, they’re gone forever.

---

我前面说的定期透析，你知道肾衰患者怎么个定期法不？

About the regular dialysis I mentioned earlier, do you know what “regular” means for kidney failure patients?

---

每礼拜三次，每次4个小时，终身的，除非换肾，你以后还想出去玩？

Three times a week, four hours each time, for a lifetime—unless you have a kidney transplant. Do you still hope to travel and go out freely in the future?

---

人家判取保候审的都比你自由；

Even people on bail pending trial have more freedom than you;

---

神经系统损伤不可逆，坏了就是坏了，眼上的断了你就看不见，腿上的断了你就不会走路。

Nerve system damage is irreversible. Once injured, it can’t be fixed. Damaged eye nerves leave you blind; damaged leg nerves leave you unable to walk.

---

那机制是让你神经旁的血管痉挛坏死进而引发神经元坏死，啥概念？

The mechanism causes blood vessels around the nerves to spasm and die, which further leads to neuron necrosis. What does that mean?

---

自体移植都做不了，动手术不慎切断了都能从腚上抽一根补上，它这个直接给你治愈的可能性根除；

Even autotransplantation won’t work. If a nerve is accidentally cut in surgery, doctors can repair it with a nerve taken from your buttocks, but mushroom poisoning completely rules out any chance of a full recovery.

---

肝损需要长期养，喝酒别想了，得别的病吃药也受限制。

Liver damage requires long-term recuperation. Drinking alcohol is out of the question, and taking medicine for other illnesses will also be restricted.

---

别人10块能解决的药，你要吃150的。

A drug that costs others 10 yuan may cost you 150 yuan.

---

不信自己回家随便找几盒常规药，看看说明书，是不是写着肾损肝损患者禁服。

If you don’t believe it, check the instruction manuals of any common medicines at home—nearly all state they are forbidden for people with kidney or liver damage.

---

懒得查？那我告诉你，肝损患者严禁所有退烧药，包括布洛芬。

Too lazy to check? Let me tell you directly: liver damage patients are strictly prohibited from taking all fever reducers, including ibuprofen.

---

别人发烧两天磕个胶囊好了，换成你，住院去吧您呐。

Others recover in two days with just one capsule when they have a fever, but for you, it’s straight to the hospital.

---

想彻底治好也不难，移植就行；

A complete cure is not impossible—only a liver transplant can do it;

---

另外还有枕页，下丘脑，海马体，没错直接伤你脑子。

It also directly damages key parts of your brain: the occipital lobe, hypothalamus and hippocampus.

---

枕页是你视觉信息处理器，这东西坏了你能看着东西却不认识或者认成别的。

The occipital lobe is your visual processing center. If it’s damaged, you can see things but fail to recognize them, or mistake them for something else.

---

你那期盼的幻觉，就是这玩意儿受攻击的结果；

The hallucinations you look forward to are exactly the result of this part being injured;

---

下丘脑是整合你神经系统和内分泌系统的，这个坏了你的垂体-甲状腺-肾上腺-性腺一路等着失调就行。

The hypothalamus coordinates the nervous and endocrine systems. Once damaged, your pituitary gland, thyroid, adrenal glands and gonads will all fall into imbalance.

---

对了它还负责控制体温，哎嘿，你还不能用退烧药；

It also controls body temperature, which means you still can’t take any fever medicine;

---

海马体是管记忆的，电视剧里那些被车创失忆的多数就是伤这了。

The hippocampus controls memory. Most TV characters who lose their memory after a car crash suffer damage here.

---

所以长期记忆力减退和视力障碍是大概率的，不过比起幻觉和长期认知障碍来说也不算啥。

So long-term memory loss and visual problems are highly likely, yet they are minor compared to persistent hallucinations and chronic cognitive impairment.

---

这个幻觉可不是你能把枕头看成女明星上去一顿啃。

These hallucinations are not the silly kind where you mistake a pillow for a celebrity and act recklessly.

---

是你半年后还不能自己上街过马路，因为你眼里的街景，会在你毫无防备的情况下打乱重排。

Instead, you still can’t cross the street alone half a year later, because the street scenery in your vision will scramble and rearrange without any warning.

---

以上所有一切的前提是，你运气足够好，那些提到的器官还没坏死；

All the misfortunes above only happen if you are lucky enough that those vital organs haven’t completely died;

---

你祖上行善积德，还给你留了条烂命。

It’s only the good karma of your ancestors that leaves you with a half-dead life.

---

我是真不明白了，有些人吃个特么预制菜跟要了命似的又是资本又是体制的；

I really can’t understand some people. They make a huge fuss over ready-made meals, blaming capital and the system endlessly;

---

喝个无糖饮料分析各种甜味剂有多大害处；

They analyze every possible harm of sweeteners in sugar-free drinks;

---

平时什么不饱和脂肪酸，优质蛋白，碳水，亚硝酸盐，微量元素矿物质研究的那叫一仔细啊。

They study unsaturated fatty acids, high-quality protein, carbs, nitrites, trace elements and minerals extremely carefully in daily life.

---

主动去吃毒物吃出中毒症状反而成了浪漫的事了。

Yet they take the initiative to eat toxic wild mushrooms, and even romanticize getting poisoned.

---

还红伞伞白杆杆，吃完一起躺板板。

They even chant the silly rhyme: red cap, white stem, lie down forever after eating them.

把死亡形容得多么可爱啊。

They make death sound so harmless and cute.

对于喝百草枯的那些人你们话术可不是这样啊。
But you never speak in such a playful way about people who drink paraquat to end their lives.

再跟你说一遍，你向往的那个要啥有啥的幻觉，叫特么症状，是你身体遭到攻击破坏之后的症状！

Let me say it again: those wonderful hallucinations you crave are nothing but poisoning symptoms, caused by your body being severely damaged!

---

不是你整点东西逗你脑子玩儿，让你体验一下新世界；

It’s not something fun to amuse your brain and let you experience a strange new world;

---

是身体好几处已经彻底坏了，在那喊老大咱要完犊子了维持不了正常认知了。

It means multiple parts of your body have broken down completely, failing to maintain normal mental cognition.

---

你家电视花屏一下你觉得那颜色挺好看，所以你就故意给电视接高压电和往里倒水试试是吗？

If your TV screen glitches and you like the weird colors, would you deliberately connect it to high voltage or pour water on it on purpose?

---

为了尝顿吃的敢把命搭上，你们比吃肥了自己待宰的猪还勇。

Risking your life just for a bite of food makes you even more reckless than pigs fattened up for slaughter.

At least those who sell a kidney to buy an iPhone get to use the phone for two or three years!
