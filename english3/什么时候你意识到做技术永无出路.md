# 什么时候你意识到做技术永无出路
When did you realize that working in tech leads to no way out forever?

---

上github,去机器学习大会看看。

Go on GitHub and take a look at machine learning conferences.

---

所有人才犹如过江之鲫，所有理论和框架都被人发现并开发。

Talented people are endless, and almost all theories and frameworks have been discovered and developed.

---

不是做技术没出路，而是大部人就是普通人，怎么蹦跶都是过普通人的一生。

It’s not that there’s no way out in tech, but most people are just ordinary folks, destined to live an ordinary life no matter how hard they struggle.

---

不是说你学会了rust，go就能屠龙了。

Learning Rust or Go won’t make you an invincible expert.

---

清楚地认识自己会过普通的生活，是对自己最好的思想准备

Truly accepting that you will lead an ordinary life is the best mental preparation for yourself.
