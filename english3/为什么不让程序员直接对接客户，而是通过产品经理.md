# Why don't programmers talk directly to clients, but go through product managers instead?

客户∶我想做个电商网站

Client: I want to build an e-commerce website.

---

客户经理∶我们采用云计算技术，基于现代云原生电商开发平台，以现代前端设计艺术，为您开发高效率，高性能，美观大方的电商网站

Account Manager: We adopt cloud computing technology, based on a modern cloud-native e-commerce development platform. With contemporary front-end design aesthetics, we will build you an efficient, high-performance, elegant and stylish e-commerce website.

---

程序员∶这个我们用wordpress+xxx插件，一个小时就可以上线

Programmer: We can just use WordPress plus some plugins, and get the site online in one hour.
